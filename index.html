About

Bespoke Lighting Consultant

SSA works in conjunction with the Interior, Architecture & Landscape Designers to create a spatial experience. We explore and understand architectural design with creative intent to achieve a seamless integration between light and space. We enhance the visual environment by bringing out the best in lighting.

Lighting features add to the design experience of a space allowing to create the emotion and feel of a place. Our lighting solution is about simplicity, responding not only to your needs but to the very nature of light and what light can achieve to make an everlasting impression.
